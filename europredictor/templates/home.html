Welcome! This Website contains graphs made from data of the UEFA EURO 2016

Here are some interesting statistics made of the collected data:


Out of all countries, England was mentioned the most and Czech Republic was mentioned the least. The wide gap between England and the other countries can be explained by us always counting the word "Enlgish" as referring to the English team, while the term often refers to the English language. So there might be quite a few falsely recognized comments about England in our database.

Russia had the biggest positive change in peoples general opinion. Croatia had the most negative change. Both of these can be explained by the press about the countries' fans. There was some negative press about Russian fans at the start of the tournament, while Croatian fans got bad press later in the tournament. Since people are also discussing about that kind of events on the soccer subreddit, our data reflects that as well.
